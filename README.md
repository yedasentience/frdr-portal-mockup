# Mockup — landing page (v1)

This is the first visual mockup of the portal's **landing page**, matching the agreed sitemap
(the 1→2→3 narrative: ① Understand · ② Explore & Take · ③ Visualize & Analyze).

## How to view it
**Just double-click `index.html`** — it opens in your web browser. Nothing to install.
(It loads Tailwind CSS and fonts from the internet, so be online the first time.)

## What this is / isn't
- ✅ A **static design draft** to judge the *look, feel, and narrative flow*.
- ✅ The headline numbers are the **real collection totals** (20,491 sessions, etc.).
- ❌ **Not wired to the database** yet — buttons/links are placeholders.
- ❌ The **site name** ("Open-Field Virtual Library") is a **placeholder** — your call to decide.

## What to look at and react to (for next session)
1. **The name** — keep, or replace with something in your voice?
2. **Look & feel** — colours (pine green + clay/terracotta on parchment) and fonts (Fraunces serif
   headings + Inter body). Too warm? Too plain? More clinical, or more inviting?
3. **The hero message** — does the opening line capture the mission the way you'd say it?
4. **The concept diagram** and the four-headings band — clear enough for a newcomer?
5. **The three-zone cards** — do the one-line explanations make the zone names self-explanatory?

Jot any reactions and we'll iterate. In the real build (Option B), this page becomes a FastAPI
template; the styling carries straight over — this is not throwaway work.
